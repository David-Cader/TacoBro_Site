Privacy policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

TacoBro Live is a personal project that generates short joke and roast clips using an AI-driven avatar, and publishes them to a single TikTok account that the operator owns and controls. This app is not a public-facing service, and it does not act on behalf of any account other than the operator's own.

Information we access

When connecting to TikTok via the Content Posting API, this app requests only the permissions needed to identify the connected account and publish video content to it (for example, account nickname and basic profile information returned by TikTok's creator info endpoint). No other TikTok data is requested or accessed.

How we use this information

Account information is used solely to confirm which TikTok account is connected and to publish video content to that account at the operator's direction. It is not used for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to publishing the operator's own content.

Data storage and retention

Access tokens and refresh tokens issued by TikTok are stored locally on the operator's own device and are not transmitted to or stored on any third-party server. Tokens are retained only as long as needed to operate the publishing pipeline and can be revoked at any time by disconnecting the app from the TikTok account settings.

Data sharing

We do not sell, share, or otherwise disclose any account information or content data to third parties.

Content

All video content, captions, and audio are created by the operator using AI-generated voice and avatar animation, and are labeled as AI-generated content in accordance with TikTok's content disclosure requirements.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to dcader64@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the project changes. The date above reflects the most recent revision.
